SolverSpace

Learn About Our Beta Solvers Team

Heritage College is a Cegep in Gatineau, QC that offers several technical programs, including Computer Science, Electrical Engineering Technology, Graphic and Web Design and Business Administration. Students of these programs learn many vital skills that enable them to contribute their community by solving complex problems.

The SolverSpace Club at Heritage College is a team of these students who want to build a portfolio of projects that prove their skills and help real people.

This club will launch on Thursday, January 29, 2025. If you want to be a Beta Seeker and have a solution personally engineered to make your life easier, contact us!
